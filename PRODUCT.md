# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Investment advisors and private-banking clients of LATAM ConsultUs. They arrive
with a specific intent — to commission analyst-produced research — and work in a
focused, professional context (desk or laptop, often alongside other market
tools). They are financially literate and expect precision; they have little
patience for friction, decoration that gets in the way, or anything that reads as
unserious. Spanish-first (es) audience across Latin America.

## Product Purpose

A client portal that turns LATAM ConsultUs's research capacity into a few-clicks
request flow. The primary loop: browse instrument categories (portfolios,
strategies, bonds, mutual funds, ETFs, equities/structured products) and request
a bespoke report (an *informe*) that is then delivered into the user's library
within each section. Success = the user finds the right instrument category and
submits a request quickly and confidently, trusting that a competent house is
behind the response. Live monitoring is secondary; the request is the job.

## Brand Personality

Institutional and precise. Calm authority earned through competence, not flash —
the trust signal is restraint, tabular accuracy, and impeccable craft. Voice is
considered, expert, and quietly confident; Spanish copy stays formal-professional
("Su mesa de análisis", usted form). Emotional goal: the user feels they are in
capable, serious hands. Closest visual altitude: **Stripe / Linear docs** — crisp
surfaces, generous whitespace, excellent typography, restrained accent color,
subtle and purposeful motion.

## Anti-references

- **Crypto / neon dark** (explicit): no neon-on-black, no glassmorphism-as-default,
  no hype aesthetic. This is the hard line.
- **Generic SaaS dashboard**: avoid the gradient-accent hero-metric template,
  endless identical icon-card grids, and the purple-startup look.
- **Retail trading app**: no gamification, confetti, or green-up/red-down hype.
- **Cluttered legacy-bank portal**: no dense, undifferentiated gray enterprise UI
  with flat hierarchy.

## Design Principles

1. **Trust through restraint.** Authority is signaled by precision and calm, not
   by decoration. When in doubt, remove rather than add.
2. **The request is the throughline.** Every screen should make "request the right
   report" obvious and low-friction; navigation and content serve that path.
3. **Precision is the aesthetic.** Tabular numerals, exact alignment, real data
   hierarchy. Numbers are first-class typographic citizens.
4. **Motion with a reason.** Subtle, fast, purposeful; it clarifies state or
   relationship, never performs. Honor reduced-motion everywhere.
5. **Quietly premium.** Generous whitespace and confident typography do the
   heavy lifting; the navy + celeste identity carries the brand without shouting.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target **WCAG AA**. Body text ≥4.5:1 contrast against its background (watch muted
gray on tinted near-white surfaces); large text ≥3:1. Visible, branded focus rings
on all interactive elements. Full keyboard navigability for the sidebar, search,
and request flows. Every animation (hero typewriter, reveals, hovers) needs a
`prefers-reduced-motion: reduce` alternative — the portal already does this for the
hero; hold all future motion to the same bar. Spanish-language content; ensure
`lang="es"` and accessible names on icon-only controls.
